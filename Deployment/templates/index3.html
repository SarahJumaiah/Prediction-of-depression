Low Depression | 0% to 35%
- Stay active and try exercises that make you feel good.
- Take time to relax and try deep breathing or meditation.
- Reach out to friends, family, or support groups for understanding and connection.
- Consider talking to a mental health professional for guidance and support.
Intermediate Depression | 36% to 65%
- Engage in activities that bring you joy and set achievable goals.
- Challenge negative thoughts and replace them with positive ones.
- Explore hobbies or creative outlets for self-expression.
- Seek support from loved ones and consider therapy if needed.
High Depression | 66% to 100%
- Seek professional help from a psychiatrist or psychologist.
- Discuss medication or other treatment options with a healthcare provider.
- Build a strong support system with loved ones.
- Prioritize self-care through sleep, nutrition, and relaxation techniques.
