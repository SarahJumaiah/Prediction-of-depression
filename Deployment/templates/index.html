SerenityScale: Depression Evaluation Test

The SerenityScale is a depression evaluation test that provides a positive, negative, or neutral result.
Based on the result, tailored recommendations are offered to assist individuals in seeking appropriate support and resources for their emotional well-being.

Low Depression

Mild depressive symptoms that may cause occasional sadness, loss of interest, and low energy.

Intermediate Depression

Moderate depressive symptoms that can lead to persistent sadness, changes in appetite and sleep, difficulty concentrating, and decreased energy levels.

High Depression

Significant depressive symptoms detected. Professional assistance strongly recommended.
